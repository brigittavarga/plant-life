About Plant Life

The florist behind

I‘ve been dreaming about opening my own flower shop since I was a kid. I am passionate about decorating big,spectecular events the same way I am about making a simple flower bouquet for mother‘s day. Through lots of failures, challenges and experiences my life finally led me to open my lovely plant shop "Plant Life" back in 2014. I am looking forward to meeting with current and future customers. Flowers make life prettier.
